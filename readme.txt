#3 Bit RGB 
- Each bit represents the presence of R, G or B.
- Allowing for 8 different colors.

# Decimal
- 000 - 0
- 001 - 1
- 010 - 2
- 011 - 3
- 100 - 4
- 101 - 5
- 110 - 6
- 111 - 7

## RGB
- 100 - Red - #F00
- 010 - Green - #0F0
- 001 - Blue - #00F

## Combined
- 110 - Yellow - #FF0
- 101 - Purple - #F0F
- 011 - Cyan - #0FF
- 000 - Black - #000
- 111 - White - #FFF
